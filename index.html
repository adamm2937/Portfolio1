01 — About

Computer Science student at Université Paris-Saclay with a deep focus on cybersecurity and intelligent security systems. Driven by a single question: how do attacks evolve, and how do we stay ahead of them. From threat detection and digital forensics to SOC operations and incident response, I work at the intersection of understanding how systems break and engineering what makes them resilient.

My approach is analytical and hands-on. I investigate attack patterns, identify root causes, and build practical detection and response tools that go beyond theory. Solutions designed to strengthen real-world infrastructure and reduce actual risk.
